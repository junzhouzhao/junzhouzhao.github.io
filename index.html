About

Junzhou Zhao received the Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Xiaohong Guan. He was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. John C.S. Lui. His research interests include large-scale graph/network measurement, mining, and optimization, with a particular interest in online social networks, a.k.a. the network science.

I am available on the job market, and interested in becoming a research scientist, data scientist, or software engineer. My CV is available upon request.

Research

network data analysis, large-scale graph measurement/mining/optimization, streaming algorithms
