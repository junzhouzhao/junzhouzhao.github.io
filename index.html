Biography

Junzhou Zhao is currently an associate professor at the School of Cyber Security, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He was a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Computer, Electrical and Mathematical Science and Engineering (CEMSE), King Abdullah University of Science and Technology, working with Prof. Xiangliang Zhang. He was also a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. John C.S. Lui. He holds a Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering, and a B.S. degree in Automation Science and Technology, both from Xi'an Jiaotong University.
